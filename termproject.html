Self Cleaning Board

Smart Board of the Future

The self-erasing board, also known as an electronic whiteboard is a device that allows you to write or draw on its surface and then erase or clear the content without the need for traditional “erasers”. Whiteboards can develop ghosting, which is the faint residue of previous markings that remain even after erasing. Ghosting can make it difficult to write or draw cleanly, as previous marks may still be visible. Staining can also occur if the markers are not completely erased, leaving behind stubborn marks that are challenging to remove. Also, Maintenance and Cleaning Whiteboards require regular maintenance and cleaning to keep them in good condition. Erasing markers may not always remove all traces, and special cleaning solutions or wipes may be needed to get rid of stubborn stains or ghosting. This additional effort and time for maintenance can be seen as a drawback compared to traditional chalkboards.
