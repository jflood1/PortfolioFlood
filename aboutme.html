Hello, Bernie here, a Graphic Design graduate from California State University Dominguez Hills (CSUDH). Prior to CSUDH, I earned my certificate from Orange Coast College, completing my This program introduced me to Digital Arts Design and Concept, along with Painting, Art History, and Basic Drawing. During my time at OCC, I received training in design principles and composition aesthetics, preparing me for higher education at the university level.

Currently residing in Medford, Oregon, I spent 3 years in Orange County and a decade in the Bay Area. Graphic Imaging has fascinated me since my youth, and although pursued later in my career, I continue to discover its immense potential. With a strong foundation in visual and art direction, I am proficient in Adobe Creative Cloud and have recently acquired skills in web development using Visual Studio Code, enhancing my abilities in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I draw inspiration from influential Graphic Designers of our time such as Fabien Baron, Bea Feistler, and Ruth Ansel, who fuel my creative passion.
